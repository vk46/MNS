About US

We are pleased to introduce MNS TOURS and TRAVELS a fastest growing conveyance solution provider in Bangalore providing transportation service as a sub vendor to Multinational Companies , IT Companies corporate sectors and Developers.

We are currently operating with a fleet size of 250+ plus vehicles consisting of Innova , Etios, Tempo Traveller, Mini Buses and Buses All our vehicles are equipped with state –of-the art modern road map of the city and its suburbs with a view to provide quick transport solutions within the shortest journey to maintain timely login.

Our drivers are well trained to take care of corporate sector & employees expectations in terms of our deliverables.

We take special care and precaution in case of female employees by way of prioritizing their transport and ensuring their safety We are also ready to provide brand new buses to extend enhanced service to our clients.

We offer highest quality and cost effective transport solutions to our clients and we stand by our motto “Customer satisfaction is our absolute satisfaction”. Our clients are appreciative of the quality and timely transport services provided by us and our prompt attention to their concerns, if any.

Our employees are our most valuable asset and their contribution is an important chapter in our success story. We constantly train and help them to cope up with the ever changing business environment.

Our mission is to become one of the leading corporate transport service provider companies in the country.
